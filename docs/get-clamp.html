Download CLAMP

CLAMP is available in two versions:


1) CLAMP-CMD, a command line program that implements the state-of-the-art techniques for extracting clinical concepts from text; and

2) CLAMP-GUI, which provides GUIs for efficiently building clinical NLP pipelines for your individual applications.

Special CLAMP licenses are available to academic users for their individual research projects. All license requests and technical support of CLAMP (for both academic and industrial users) are now handled by Melax Technologies Inc. Please fill up the form here ( https://clamp.melaxtech.com/get-clamp.php ) to submit your download request. We will send you the download link to the version you are requesting and activation code after review, which typically takes 2-3 business days.
